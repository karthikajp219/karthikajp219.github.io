Mildura 29-Dec-2018

  • 8 AM start

    Final check is done, fuel air and water is and everything we need and more things we don’t need as well. We are on time to start this trip. Its how we planned to spend our december break. And its all happening. First big road trip for her and its bigger than last one for me.

  • 10 Dog in tucker box

    This is our first stop and had to stop for a picture. The Dog on the Tuckerbox is a monument and tourist attraction, few miles from Gundagai. It has a wishing pool and we did wish for a safe and fun trip.

  • 1230 Narrandera  Lincoln Oakbank Brewery historic tower

    It almost lunch time and before that we happen to spot an abandoned tower Lincoln Oakbank Brewery. It is a majestic tower and can be seen anywhere from the tower. Took a quick snack break before lunch and headed towards our way.

  • 0230 Hay , Bidgee Riverside Trail

    before we starve to death we need to stop for lunch and the temperature was soaring high that we went to the Bidgee Riverside Trail. I remembered this place was full of water in 2015 the last time I visited but now it’s with less water and there are few water ski sports happening and water is poisoned with algae. with bit of disappointment we had our lunch  and did a small trail walk and took some pictures and off we go.

  • Lunch Break

    Yummy Lamb Rice, filled us like heaven

  • 0436 Balranald

    Our next pit stop was town Balranald, a small town hardly any people , we started feeling the glimpse of outback.

  • 0530 Robinvale, Euston  grapes

    before we cross the border we spotted fresh Grapes farms and couldn’t resist from getting don and taking some pictures

  • 0700 Mildura

    Finally reached the accommodation , too hot nothing else mattered to us except the pool without wasting much time we cooled off  jumping in to it.
