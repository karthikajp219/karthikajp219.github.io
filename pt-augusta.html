  • car coffee

    everyone needs a coffee to get their mornings , so does our car.

  • 1030 Australias two longest rivers Meet

    not often rivers merge in to another before it hits the sea, here 20 minutes from Mildura is the junction island where Murray and Darling merges in to one. That spectalur view and slightly noticible different colors of water is must to be place. Quite with lots of picinic spots and relaxing place to spen you morning.

  • 1130 Perry Sandhills

    Not very far from junction is perry sand hills where the wind is the artist and makes it picture on the huge sand canvas.  People rolling from the hills and bunch of the sand boarding it’s a fun place and you sure to get best pictures here.

  • 1250 SA Border

    after a 100 km drive we are driving  from victoria to SA . it is not a big deal for us  but for  fruits an veggies it is, yes we cant take fresh organic stuff in to south australia. you must eat or dispose of fruit or fruiting vegetables before travelling through the Fruit Fly Exclusion Zone.You must stop at quarantine stations where you will be checked for any fresh fruit, vegetables, other plants or plant products. Don't risk a fine! Plant health inspectors can search your vehicle at a quarantine station and take any restricted items.You must dispose of all restricted items in the disposal bins.

  • 0310 Morgan

    The first town we stopped after crossing the border was morgan , you sure need to stop for refuel.

  • 0430 spalding

    after a 400 Km drive everyone needs a break and we saw some civilisation and hit our breaks. Spalding is a very small town with lots and lots of farm lands. Houses were really good with neat grass yards and scenary perfect fron yards. Refuel and off we go.

  • 0900 port augusta sunset

    It getting dark here and we were planning to hit Pt.Augusta before the sunset so no plan on stopping elsewhere , and yes we did it.  Sunset , beech and people you love you couldn’t ask for more.
